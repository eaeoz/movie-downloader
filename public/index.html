Android iOS

Web (No Sign-In)

Browse popular movies without an account:

letterboxd.com/films/popular/this/week/

Your Watchlist

Find movies you want to watch. First set your Letterboxd username in the Settings panel, then visit:

letterboxd.com/yourusername/watchlist/

After saving your username, click the refresh button to load your watchlist into the dropdown above.

How It Works

  1. Install the Letterboxd app on your phone and add movies to your watchlist.
  2. Enter your Letterboxd username in Settings and save.
  3. Click refresh to sync your watchlist into the dropdown above.
  4. Select a movie – Auto mode downloads the best quality, Manual lets you pick.
